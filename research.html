02 · Research Paper

Governance Under Load

Academic paper · Draft · May 2026

Why Principal-Agent Systems Require Structural Governance, Not Relational Oversight

The technical paper. Presents Terminal-Bench 2.0 results on 89 coding tasks, isolates the governance contribution from model capability, and proposes a framework for measuring governance overhead.

Key result: 6 governance rules improved the same model from 42% to 67.4% on Terminal-Bench, a 25-point lift. Rules 1, 3, and 4 (read first, iterate don’t repeat, verify before done) carried most of the improvement.

The 6 rules

What carried the 25 points

Genesis (read first), Plan First, Iterate Don’t Repeat, Verify Before Done, Time Is Limited, When Stuck (change approach). Testing showed rules 1, 3, and 4 drove most of the lift.
